# Employee Handbook

**CONFIDENTIAL - Manager Access Required**

Last updated: January 2024

## Compensation Structure

### Salary Bands

| Level | Title | Salary Range |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| L1 | Junior Engineer | $80,000 - $110,000 |
| L2 | Engineer | $110,000 - $150,000 |
| L3 | Senior Engineer | $150,000 - $200,000 |
| L4 | Staff Engineer | $200,000 - $280,000 |
| L5 | Principal Engineer | $280,000 - $400,000 |

### Bonus Structure

- Annual bonus target: 15-25% of base salary
- Performance multiplier: 0.5x to 2.0x based on review
- Stock refresh grants awarded at L3 and above

## Performance Review Process

Reviews are conducted quarterly with the following rating scale:
- **Exceeds Expectations**: Top 15% performers, eligible for accelerated promotion
- **Meets Expectations**: Core performers, standard merit increase
- **Needs Improvement**: Performance improvement plan required
- **Does Not Meet**: Separation may be considered

## Promotion Criteria

Promotions require:
1. Minimum 12 months at current level
2. Manager recommendation
3. Calibration committee approval
4. Budget availability

## Termination Procedures

Voluntary termination requires 2-week notice. Severance packages:
- Less than 1 year: 2 weeks
- 1-3 years: 4 weeks
- 3+ years: 8 weeks plus 1 week per additional year
